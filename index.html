About

刘玥含 (Liu Yuehan)

Animation 3D Arts UI Design Interaction

Education: Xiamen University

Currently, I plan to continue my studies in China, focusing on digital media art and interaction design. I'm interested in how digital media can express social emotions and individual behaviors, and aim to integrate interaction design, media communication, and technology to address social issues.
我计划在国内继续深造，专注于数字媒体艺术与交互设计。关注数字媒介如何表达社会情感与个体行为，期望在未来的学术环境中提升研究与技术能力，将交互设计、媒介传播与技术相结合，服务于社会议题的表达与创新设计。
